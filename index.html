The White House Tweets

Hello, Twitter! It's Barack. Really! Six years in, they're finally giving me my own account.

— President Obama (@POTUS) May 18, 2015

In October, Jimmy Kimmel selected some tweets sent by people about President Obama and recorded the President’s response. Here at the Laboratory for Social Machines, within the MIT Media Lab, we flipped Kimmel’s bit, selected some tweets sent by the President, and recorded how the American people responded. What did we find? No mean tweets, but several [policy-heavy tweets and sparsely engaged citizenry.]

Drawing from areas of computer science including machine learning, natural language processing, and network analysis, we explored how America responded to tweets from President Barack Obama (@potus), First Lady Michelle Obama (@flotus), and the White House (@whitehouse). Our algorithms categorized each tweet by issue, demography, and geography. We summarized the top tweeted issues by the President and America’s responses. Explore the replies yourself below!
